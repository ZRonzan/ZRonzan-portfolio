Code-to-Memory

JavaScrip | Python | SQLAlchemy | React

Code-to-Memory is a simple and elegant one page application aiming to provide a place for users to organize their study materials and level up their recall of coding topics. they can create and organize classes, decks and cards to tailor their learning however they see fit, with a mastery system that can allow them to track their understanding at a glance.

Legendhood

JavaScrip | Python | SQLAlchemy | React

A stock trading simulation app using live data for many assets commonly found on stock trading apps like Robinhood. Users can "buy" and "sell" stocks and watch their simulated portfolio grow (or not) with changes in the market. (not an actual financial trading app. Stocks bought and sold are simulated and do not reflect actual stock trading)
